2. ACTIVITY TRACKER
It is not always possible to keep track of the work done by the remote workers in person.so build a activity tracker for such employees so that the leaders can get all the details of the work done by employee and whether he is really doing the work or not and for how many hours he is working.
3. COMPUTER MONITORING

4. EXPENSE MANAGER

It should also provide you with your current account balance after all the transaction.
